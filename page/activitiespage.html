OUR ACTIVITIES

Over the years, Kuramo has participated in numerous activities organised by goverments and private sectors. Below are some of it.

Yellow Cowries programme

On the 11th of November, 2022. Some of our students participated in the Yellow Cowries programme. The programme aimed at enlighten the students on financial literacy and economy wise. after a long term learning from the foundation, a competion was held where one of our student named George Peter emegerged the winner and was awarded a plague.

I.T competition

On the 4th of may 2022, seven of our students: John Theresa, Sabastine Paul, Nnadi Franklin, Usman Abubakar, Elijah Marcia, Sunday Deborah and David Emmanuel participated in an I.T competition held by Axon Analytics (a digital market based company). After their successful participation, Usman Abubakar emerged the winner and was awarded prizes and an opportunity to work with the company as an intern where he learns coding.

Education U.S.A

The American corner in partnership with Ovie Brume Foundation organised a program on the 5th of May, 2022. The program was specifically organised for Kuramo Senior College and Victoria Island Secondary School. The program aimed at exposing the students to various scholarship abroad and as well encouraged them to strive for excellent grades that can fetch them scholarship within and outside Nigeria.

Project icommunity

On the 27th of june 2023, the project icommunity challenge was featured into Kuramo Senior College, victoria Island, Lagos. initially, 10 groups of 5 students each were formed. The group were tasked to solve a feasible problems within their society and the 2 groups with the best solution were selected to compete with students of other school which they did and qualified for final. In the final stage, kuramo senior college was selected as 2nd best with a total prize of N500,000.
